# Research Brief Template

## Question

Write the decision or investigation question in one sentence.

## Key Findings

List the evidence that most changes the decision.

## Tradeoffs

Compare the practical consequences of each option. Prefer behavior, cost, risk, maintenance, and user impact over generic pros and cons.

## Recommendation

Choose one path when the evidence is strong enough. If the evidence is not strong enough, say what must be checked first.

## Next Steps

List the smallest actions that would reduce uncertainty or move the work forward.
